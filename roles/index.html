Hero Roles

Each role has a unique job in the team. Understanding your role is the
first step to mastering the battlefield and achieving victory.

🛡️

Tank

INITIATOR & MEATSHIELD

The frontline of the team. Tanks soak up massive amounts of damage and initiate team fights to create space for allies.
